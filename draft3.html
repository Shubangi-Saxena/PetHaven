About us
PetHaven is a non-profit, non-organization setup in 2021. We are currently located at Banashankari, Bangalore. We are expanding our organization at the moment. Even as a newly established organization, we have helped a lot of pets find their way back to there owners. Our website helps you find your lost pet wherever they are. Our scope only includes cats and dogs at the moment. We also offer you to adopt pets that are with us.
MISSION
Our mission is very simple. No one wants to lose their pet. But, it happens. We are there to help you find your pet no matter what! We want to create a better world for animals and the people who love and need them. And it all begins with love.
